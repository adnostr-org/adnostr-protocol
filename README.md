# AdNostr Protocol 🚀
**AI-powered decentralized advertising protocol for Nostr. Reducing cross-border ad costs via automated content generation and Lightning Network micro-payments.**

---

### 🌍 The Problem
Traditional cross-border advertising (Google/Amazon) is broken:
- **High Intermediary Fees**: Platforms take 30-50% "gatekeeper tax".
- **Slow Settlements**: International payments take weeks to process.
- **Data Privacy**: Centralized tracking invades user privacy.

### 💡 Our Solution
AdNostr is a decentralized incentive layer for global commerce:
- **AI-Native Creatives**: Automated localization of ad content via our proprietary AI API (e.g., localized beauty & goods visuals).
- **Nostr-Based Attribution**: Transparent, P2P performance tracking (Views/Clicks) using NIP tags.
- **Instant Lightning Payouts**: Real-time $Sats rewards for relayers and creators via Bitcoin Lightning Network.

---

### 🧠 The Mathematical Framework (v0.1)
To ensure fair distribution and prevent spam, AdNostr implements a **Game-Theoretic Incentive Model**:

#### 1. Reward Distribution Formula
$$R = \frac{C \times \ln(I + 1)}{D^k}$$
*Reward ($R$) is weighted by Conversion ($C$), Interaction ($I$), and Propagation Distance ($D$).*

#### 2. AI Aesthetic & Trend Ranking
$$Score = \alpha \cdot E + \beta \cdot T$$
*Optimizing ROI by ranking Image Entropy ($E$) and Trend Alignment ($T$).*
